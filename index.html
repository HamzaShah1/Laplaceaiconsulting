What we offer

Our services

Each service is available as a standalone engagement or as part of a broader programme. Not sure which one fits? Our discovery tool will guide you.

01AI Strategy & Roadmap
Strategy

A structured assessment of where your organisation stands with AI, followed by a prioritised roadmap of initiatives ranked by commercial impact and feasibility. Delivered as a board-ready document with clear next steps and success metrics.

02Workflow Automation
Automation

We identify and automate high-friction, repetitive processes in your business. Reducing manual overhead, cutting turnaround times, and freeing your team to focus on work that requires human judgement.

03LLM Integration
Integration

From customer-facing AI assistants to internal productivity agents, we design and deploy large language model solutions that integrate cleanly into your existing products and workflows, built to production standard from day one.

04RAG & Knowledge Systems
Integration

We build retrieval-augmented generation systems that make your organisation's documents, data, and institutional knowledge instantly queryable in plain language, by your staff, your tools, or your customers.

05Team Training & Enablement
Enablement

Practical programmes that equip your people to understand and use AI confidently. From executive briefings on what AI can and cannot do, to hands-on technical workshops for the teams who will operate systems day to day.

06Build vs. Buy Advisory
Advisory

Independent, vendor-agnostic guidance on whether to build a proprietary AI solution, adopt an off-the-shelf product, or pursue a hybrid approach. We have no commercial relationships with software vendors so our advice is entirely in your interest.
